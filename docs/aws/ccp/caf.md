# AWS Cloud Adoption Framework

## Perspectives

### Business
Ensure our cloud investments accelerate your digital transformation and business outcomes.

#### Stakeholders
* CEO
* CFO
* COO
* CIO
* CTO

??? info "Business Perspectives"

    #### Strategy Management
    * Consider how the cloud can support your long-term goals.
    * Identify opportunities to retire tech debt.
    * Consider reach to new customers, new market segments.
    * Prioritize strategic objectives and evolve strategy over time.

    #### Portfolio Management
    * Deliver the right cloud products at the right time.
    * *Leverage automated discovery tools and 7 migration strategies.*
    * Consider short-term and long-term outcomes.

    #### Innovation Management 
    * Focus on optimization of existing products, processes, experiences.
    * Develop an end-to-end process for scaling successful pilots.

    #### Product Management
    * Organize teams around products for agility.
    * Product roadmaps, end-to-end lifecycles, associated value streams.

    #### Strategic Partnership
    * Partner with your cloud provider (consulting, managed services).
    * Leverage promotional credits, funding benefits, marketplace.

    #### Data Monetization
    * Develop a long-term data monetization strategy.
    * Leverage data and analytics to improve operations, customer and employee experience.

    #### Business Insights
    * "Big picture" first, then drill down into details
    * Statistics, visualization, communication

    #### Data Science
    * Leverage experimentation, advanced analytics and machine learning to solve complex business problems.
    * Mitigate risk by delegating low-confidence predictions to humans.

### People
Focus on culture, organizational structure, leadership, workforce.

#### Stakeholders
* CIO
* COO
* CTO
* Cloud Director
* Enterprise Leaders

??? info "People Perspectives"

    #### Culture Evolution
    * Evaluation, evolve, codify organizational culture with digital transformation aspirations
    * Consider rapid transformation, agile, cross-functional teams.

    #### Transformational Leadership
    * Leaders must put on equal focus on people and tech
    * Clear + consisten communication to align organization
    * Consider a transofmration office (Cloud Center of Excellence)

    #### Cloud Fluency
    * Hire, develop, train, motivate a high-performing workforce.
    * Have a well-constructed strategy for cloud skills

    #### Workforce Transformation
    * Modernize roles to attract talent
    * Aim for diverse and inclusive workforce with a mix of technical and non0technical skills

    #### Change Acceleration
    * Define what success looks like early on in the journey
    * Identify stakeholders, risks, dependencies and barriers
    * Make changes stick with feedback (rewards, recognition)

    #### Organization Design
    * Reflect if the organization design fits your goals
    * Consider a centralized team to facilitate your cloud initiative

    #### Organization Alignment
    * Prioritize business outcomes, agility, rpoduct/service innovation
    * Set measurable goals, mechanisms for cloud adoption

### Governance
Orchestrate cloud initiatives, maximize organization benefit, minimize transformation risks.

#### Stakeholders
* Chief Transformation Officer
* CIO
* CTO
* CFO
* Chief Data Officer
* Chief Risk Officer

??? info "Governance Perspectives"

    #### Program and Project Management
    * Align initiatives for optimized/integrated costs, effort, benefits
    * Validation your roadmap with business sponsors
    * Agile apporach to minimize far reaching predictions

    #### Benefits Management
    * Clearly identify benefits of cloud
    * Prioritize cloud benefits and track progress

    #### Risk Management
    * Identify operational risks (infrastructure availability, security)
    * Reduce need for large upfront infra costs

    #### Cloud Financial Management
    * Use best pricing models
    * Clarify financial roles + responsibilities, shared understanding
    * Align account structure & tagging strategy with organization
    * Ensure worklaods are well-architected, cost effective
    * Centralize management of on-premise and cloud software licenses

    #### Application Portfolio Management
    * Link applications to their associated resources
    * Start with crtitical apps, define business capabilities, map them to their resources
    * Build a complete picture of each app
    
    #### Data Governance
    * Data owners
    * Data lifecycle policies
    * Prioritize data quality
    
    #### Data Curation
    * Collection, organize, enrich metadata to create a data catalog
    
### Platform
Build enterprise-grade, scalable, hybrid cloud platform. Modernize existing workloads, implement new cloud native solutions.

#### Stakeholders
* CTO
* Tech leaders
* Architects
* Engineers

??? info "Platform Perspectives"
    
    #### Platform Architecture
    * Define best practice blueprints & guardrails
    * Authentication, security, netowrking, logging & monitoring
    * Evaluate hybrid cloud when on-premise still has a need

    #### Data Architecture
    * Reduce complexity, cost, technical debt
    * Select key tech for architectural layers, ingestion, storing
    * Simplify with serverless
    * Adopt a Lake House when necessary

    #### Platform Engineering
    * Deploy best practice blueprints, detective and preventive guardrails
    * Automate account provisioning workflow
    * Set up connection between on-premise and cloud

    #### Data Engineering
    * Automate data and analytic platforms
    * Share blueprints with business analysts/data scientists (self-service)

    #### Provisioning and Orchestration
    * Streamline this process
    * Self-service for publishing, distributing, browsing, consuming

    #### Modern App Development
    * Containers, serverless, microservices, event-driven architecture
    * Modernize, replatform, refactoring

    #### CI/CD
    * Automate CI/CD, manual to Production
    * Deployment strategies; in-place, rolling, immutable, blue-green

### Security
Achieve confidentiality, integrity, availability of data.

#### Stakeholders
* Chief Information Security Officer
* Chief Compliance Officer
* Audit leaders
* Architects
* Engineers

??? info "Security Perspectives"

    #### Security Governance
    * Develop, maintain and effectively communicate security roles.
    * Laws, rules, regulations, standards/frameworks

    #### Security Assurance
    * Continuously monitor & evaluate environment
    * Audit reports, compliance certs

    #### Identity And Access Management
    * Create and control identities
    * Use MFA
    * Use the principal of least privilege

    #### Threat Detection
    * Monitor all environments
    * Identify security misconfigurations

    #### Vulnerability Management
    * Scan for vulnerabilities
    * Red teaming, penetration testing

    #### Infrastructure Protection
    * Create network layers
    * Security groups, network access control lists, firewalls

    #### Data Protection
    * Classify your data based on criticality and sensitivity
    * Data protection controls, lifecycle management

    #### Application Security
    * Automate security-related tasks
    * Vulnerability scans

    #### Incident Response
    * Educate security operations and incident response teams
    * Develop runbooks, a library of incident response mechanisms
    * Simulate security events

### Operations
Cloud services meet business needs

#### Stakeholders

??? info "Operations Perspectives"

    #### Observability
    * Telemetry (logs, metrics, traces)
    * Synthetic monitoring tocreate canaries (scheduled scripts)

    * Focus on priority events, filter noise

    * Define escalation paths

    * Post-incident analyses

    #### Change and Release Management
    * Automate approval workflows
    * Use deployment management systems
    * Frequent small, reversible changes

    #### Performance Capacity Management
    * Collect and review performance data
    * Create baselines for future comparison

    #### Configuration Management
    * Define/enforce a tagging schema
    * IaC
    * Set config baselines, track with version control

    #### Patch Management
    * Apply important updates during your maintainance window
    * Update machine images, perform critical fixes asap

    #### Availability and Continuity Management
    * Cloud backup solutions
    * Disaster recovery, multi-AZ, multi region
    * Chaos engineering

    #### Application Management
    * Automate discovery of app components and resources

## Phases

### Envision
Identify and prioritize transformation opportunities across each transformation domain in line with your business objectives.

Focus on demonstrating how the cloud will help accelerate business outcomes.

### Align
Focus on identifying capability gaps across the AWS CAF perspectives.

Create strategies for identified gaps.

### Launch
Delivers pilot initiatives in production.

Demonstrate incremental business value.

Pilots should be highly impactful.

### Scale
Expand production pilots and business value to desired scale
